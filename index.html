Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart

$42

The salted caramel chocolate tart is a rich, chocolatey dessert full of flavor! Composed of a crunchy cocoa shortbread filled with a chocolate almond cream (frangipane), a delicious layer of salted caramel sauce and a generous and creamy chocolate ganache. It is truly the dessert for all chocolate lovers!

Sweet Brown Butter Rum Cannoli

$11

This homemade Italian brown butter rum cannoli is a doozy! Homemade cannoli filled with rum-soaked yellow butter cake crumbles and whipped vanilla buttercream frosting. To load onto the deliciousness, it is also drissled with sweet milky chocolate and coated with almond and apple crumble. A true delight!
